Site Reference Guide
Lexington Green
Visit Time: 30–45 min
Admission: Free
Summary: Opening battle site of the American Revolution.
Historical Source: https://www.lexingtonhistory.org
Buckman Tavern
Visit Time: 45–60 min
Admission: Required
Summary: Militia gathering place before the Battle of Lexington.
Historical Source: https://www.battlefields.org/visit/heritage-sites/buckman-tavern
Hancock-Clarke House
Visit Time: 45–60 min
Admission: Required
Summary: Paul Revere warned Samuel Adams and John Hancock here.
Historical Source: https://www.lexingtonhistory.org/hancock-clarke-house
Battle Road Trail
Visit Time: 2–3 hrs
Admission: Free
Summary: Historic route of British retreat and colonial resistance.
Historical Source: https://www.nps.gov/mima
Hartwell Tavern
Visit Time: 30–45 min
Admission: Free
Summary: Restored 18th-century tavern.
Historical Source: https://www.nps.gov/mima/hartwell-tavern.htm
Old North Bridge
Visit Time: 30–60 min
Admission: Free
Summary: Site of the “shot heard round the world.”
Historical Source: https://www.nps.gov/mima
Old Manse
Visit Time: 60–90 min
Admission: Tour Admission
Summary: Linked to Emerson and Hawthorne.
Historical Source: https://thetrustees.org/place/the-old-manse/
Orchard House
Visit Time: 60–90 min
Admission: Required
Summary: Louisa May Alcott wrote Little Women here.
Historical Source: https://louisamayalcott.org
Sleepy Hollow Cemetery
Visit Time: 45–60 min
Admission: Free
Summary: Authors Ridge burial location.
Historical Source: https://concordma.gov/1956/Sleepy-Hollow-Cemetery
Walden Pond
Visit Time: 1.5–3 hrs
Admission: State fee may apply.
Summary: Thoreau's famous retreat.
Historical Source: https://www.mass.gov/locations/walden-pond-state-reservation
Historic Deerfield
Visit Time: 3–5 hrs
Admission: Museum Admission
Summary: Preserved colonial village.
Historical Source: https://www.historic-deerfield.org
Nantasket Beach
Visit Time: 2–6 hrs
Admission: Free
Summary: Historic South Shore beach destination.
Historical Source: https://www.hull-ma.gov
Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum
Visit Time: 2–3 hrs
Admission: Museum Admission
Summary: Venetian-style museum with world-famous collection.
Historical Source: https://www.gardnermuseum.org
Museum of Fine Arts Boston
Visit Time: 3–5 hrs
Admission: Museum Admission
Summary: One of the premier art museums in America.
Historical Source: https://www.mfa.org
Beacon Hill
Visit Time: 1–2 hrs
Admission: Free
Summary: Historic Boston neighborhood.
Historical Source: https://www.thefreedomtrail.org
Bunker Hill Monument
Visit Time: 45–90 min
Admission: Free
Summary: Memorial to the Battle of Bunker Hill.
Historical Source: https://www.nps.gov/bost
Boston Common
Visit Time: 30–60 min
Admission: Free
Summary: America's oldest public park.
Historical Source: https://www.thefreedomtrail.org
Paul Revere House
Visit Time: 30–45 min
Admission: Required
Historical Source: https://www.paulreverehouse.org
Old North Church
Visit Time: 45–60 min
Admission: Tour Admission
Historical Source: https://oldnorth.com
Old South Meeting House
Visit Time: 45–60 min
Admission: Museum Admission
Historical Source: https://www.thefreedomtrail.org
Adams National Historical Park
Visit Time: 2–3 hrs
Admission: Seasonal Admission
Historical Source: https://www.nps.gov/adam
Stone Library
Visit Time: 20–30 min
Admission: Included with Tour
Historical Source: https://www.nps.gov/places/stone-library.htm
Plymouth Rock
Visit Time: 15–30 min
Admission: Free
Historical Source: https://www.pilgrimhall.org
Mayflower II
Visit Time: 60–90 min
Admission: Required
Historical Source: https://plimoth.org
